About Me

Nishchay Harpalani is a graphic designer based in India, crafting clean, modern visuals for brands and digital creators. With experience across multiple client projects, he focuses on social media creatives, branding assets, and UI-inspired layouts that are both visually appealing and intuitive to use. As a freelancer, Nishchay enjoys collaborating closely with clients, turning rough ideas into polished designs that help them stand out online and communicate clearly with their audience.
